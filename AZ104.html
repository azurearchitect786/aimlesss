Why Take AZ-104?

The AZ-104 certification validates your ability to manage Azure resources and services. It helps you:

  • ⚙️ Manage Azure identities and governance
  • 💾 Implement and manage storage
  • 🖥️ Deploy and manage Azure compute resources
  • 🌐 Configure and manage virtual networking
  • 🔒 Monitor and back up Azure resources
